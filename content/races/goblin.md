## Race Information

### Society

Goblins are very rarely a kind and compassionate sort. While power and status is important it's often gained through back stabbing and cheating, this includes close family and friends. Even if a goblin feels sorrow at the loss of another its often overshadowed by the thought of whatever they left behind.

Status among goblins can be a fickle thing and as such a constantly shifting affair. One day you're the strongest in the cave and the rightful leader of the raiding parties, the next you're recovering from a nasty wound that leaves you at the wayside without a second thought.

Despite how seemingly disorganized goblins may appear their tribes hold a not insignificant amount of duties. Hunting, foraging, raiding, and stealing to name a few. Perhaps one of the most important is the warg tenders for what is a tribe without a fierce mount to ride into battle-

When a powerful figure such as a bugbear or hobgoblin is absent from a goblin tribe their most powerful or occasionally their smartest male takes the lead. A tribal leader is an alluring role for goblins, you have first pickings after a raid and often tribute is offered for safety.

### Regions

##### Cragmaw Tribe

Nestled within the southern Neverwinter Woods rests Cragmaw Castle, a derelict fortification that serves as the home for the similarly named Cragmaw tribe. Composed primarily of goblinoids and other brutish races this tribe sends raiding parties all along the High Road.

##### Skullport

A sizable cadre of goblins work as deck hands and porters for the underground trade in Skullport. Though he once slaves these choice few have been freed and given skills and a wage enough to live a somewhat normal life. Though their days appear calm and simple these few goblins existed as drow slaves prior, surely they still know their way around a blade and find the dark of shadows comforting.

##### Sword Mountains

Snow goblins live in cold hilly regions high above the normal range of common folk. They often find themselves doing the bidding of larger beings such as Ice Trolls and Frost Giants. Though poorly equipped for proper ambushes the icy climate offers plenty of opportunity to cause avalanches, a thing these goblins delight in greatly.

### Appearance

**Small: ** Most goblins stand between three to three and a half feet tall with many cases seeming shorter still. Their small size makes them adept at hiding under all manner of underbrush, shrubbery, and village fences.

**Earthen Tones:** Ranging from yellow to red and most shades in between as well as green and dull brown hues goblin clans can appear fairly diverse. Though slight variation is possible most members of a clan will have similar coloration.

**A Goblin's Stench:** The stench of a goblin den is a thing not so easily forgotten. Poor hygiene and discarded food waste is merely the beginning of their fetid problem, fingers with rings still attached and scraps of flesh with intricate tattoos could also easily find their way into a goblin lord's hoard.

#### Varied Appearances

**Snow Bound Kin:** Those who travel the highest mountain peaks littered with snow and ice occasionally mention seeing white furred goblinoids mucking about the cliffsides. More aggressive and slightly dimmer, these northern goblinoids are ambush predators who often signal their attacks with vocal cries.

### Faith

#### Maglubiyet

The High Chieftain is the deity of all goblinkind, through order and war Maglubiyet seats himself above the others at the throne of Clangor. When he sees fit to commune with his followers Maglubiyet will send signs in blood or occasionally going so far as to speak through his shamans.

#### Bargrivyek

Though the goblins lack a proper deity devoted to kindness and compassion they do have one of unity and cooperation. Bargrivyek may not be nice or kind but his shaman promotes cooperation and cohesion needed to maintain territory.

#### Bane

Bane is a cruel and sadistic god of order and war. Though his clergy have a fierce reputation in the harshest of groups they have come far from senseless murders and sadists.
