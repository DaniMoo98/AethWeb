#### Racial Perks

**Spider Climb**
This creature may climb at full speed and does not require appropriate footing/support to do so.

**Animal Form**
This creature gains access to the small animal form druid wild shape and one additional charge of wild shape. Your animal form is a small monkey.

 
For all wildshape form uses, including non-combat, players are required to activate the Wildshape ability in RPR. You are able to recover wildshape charges spent on your small form only.

**Hengeyokai Shapeshift**
This creature has two alternative visages that they can wear apart from their animal form. They have ONE "hybrid" form that is an anthropomorphic version of their animal form and ONE human form.

 
These forms are restricted to 1 fixed appearance upon whitelisting.

**Additional Feat**
This creature gets an additional feat at creation.

This creature has access to the fluff spell Minor Illusion.

Monkey Hengeyokai have the ability to shift between a human form, a monkey form, and a hybrid form.
