# THE WORLD OF AETHRYA

### THE SWORD COAST NORTH

Welcome to **Aethrya**, a high-fantasy roleplay experience set within the legendary Sword Coast North. Whether you are a seasoned adventurer from the bustling streets of Neverwinter or a newcomer seeking fortune in the mysterious Lost Hills, your journey begins here.

Aethrya offers a deeply immersive world with unique systems for combat, professions, and character progression. Explore our comprehensive guides and rules to prepare for the challenges ahead.
