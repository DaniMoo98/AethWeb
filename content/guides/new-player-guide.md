# NEW PLAYER GUIDE

Welcome to Aethrya RP. This page is the fast path from first join to approved character.

## 1) Start Here

1. Read server rules first.
2. Read setting overview and seasonal story.
3. Pick a race, class, and profession concept that fits the setting.
4. Join Discord and submit whitelist through tickets.

## 2) Whitelist Process

Whitelist exists to keep roleplay quality consistent and lore-aligned.

- Keep your concept clear and focused.
- Match your race/class/profession to server documentation.
- Show how your character fits the current setting.
- Use complete answers in your application; avoid one-line responses.

## 3) Character Creation Checklist

- Character name and short concept.
- Chosen race and why it fits.
- Chosen class and intended subclass path.
- Chosen profession (if applicable).
- Personality, goals, and likely conflicts.
- Basic hooks for other players to engage with.

## 4) Discord Ticket System for Whitelist Approvals

Use this setup if you are building the Discord workflow for your staff team.

### Required Channels

- `#start-here` (read-only onboarding text and links)
- `#whitelist-info` (requirements and examples)
- `#open-ticket` (ticket panel button only)
- `#staff-whitelist-log` (ticket transcripts/status posts)

### Required Roles

- `@Moderator`
- `@Whitelist Team` (or reuse Moderator if small team)
- `@Applicant` (optional helper role)

### Ticket Categories

- `Whitelist - New`
- `Whitelist - Revision Requested`
- `Whitelist - Approved`
- `Whitelist - Denied`

### Ticket Form Fields

- Discord handle
- Character name
- Race
- Class / intended subclass
- Profession
- Backstory summary
- Character goals
- Consent to rules acknowledgement

### Workflow

1. Applicant clicks panel button in `#open-ticket`.
2. Bot opens private ticket channel and posts form prompt.
3. Applicant submits required fields.
4. Staff reviews for lore fit, rules fit, and RP quality.
5. Staff responds with:
   - Approved: assign whitelist role and move ticket to approved category.
   - Revision Requested: list exact edits needed and move category.
   - Denied: close with concise reason and reapply guidance.
6. Log transcript to `#staff-whitelist-log`.

### Recommended Bot Options

- Use a ticket bot that supports:
  - custom forms/modals
  - role-based access
  - transcript export
  - button panel creation

If your bot supports automations, auto-apply labels/tags like `whitelist`, `revision`, and `approved`.

## 5) After Approval

- Finalize sheet according to approved concept.
- Re-check class, spell, and profession pages before first session.
- Enter roleplay with a play-to-lift mindset.
