# STAFF STRUCTURE

Staff exists to support a fair, stable, and story-rich environment.

## Leadership Roles

### Founder

- Final authority on long-term direction and high-impact decisions.
- Escalation path when lower-level resolution is not appropriate.

### Administrator

- Oversees staff operations and cross-team execution.
- Coordinates planning, staffing, and policy consistency.

## Community Operations

### Moderator

- Frontline support for players and tickets.
- Handles general inquiries, whitelist flow, and moderation concerns.

### Trial Moderator

- In-training moderator role.
- Supports ticket flow and team operations under supervision.

## Narrative Operations

### Dungeon Master

- Runs events and narrative arcs.
- Builds world pressure, hooks, and story opportunities for players.

## Technical / Platform Roles

### System Developer

- Maintains game systems, scripts, and implementation integrity.

### Website Manager

- Maintains documentation site structure, content routes, and updates.

## If You Want To Join Staff

- Demonstrate reliability, consistency, and rules knowledge.
- Maintain calm communication under pressure.
- Show play-to-lift behavior in both RP and OOC spaces.
- Apply through the staff process when recruitment opens.

## Staff Operating Principles

- Be transparent when possible.
- Be consistent in rule enforcement.
- Document decisions in tickets.
- Escalate early when conflicts or ambiguity appear.
