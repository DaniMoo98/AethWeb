## Description

Effects You use the weave to conjure an object no larger than 1 tile and no heavier than 20 pounds. You cannot conjure any living thing nor any rpr item you do not have on your person. The item conjured is magical and cannot leave the possession of the owner. Any damaged dealt to the item will cause it to disappear. You can have a maximum of 2 items conjured at once. If cast again, it replaces the item first conjured. Allowed Examples: Chair Parchment and quil A Flower Generic Mask Small Instrument Not Allowed Examples: Specific items that belong to another (X deities relic, key to X clans door, etc.) This fluff spell cannot be used during combat and has no mechanical benefit Items with complex or moving parts cannot be conjured (gears, wheels, clocks, etc)
