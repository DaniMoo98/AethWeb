## Description

### Effects

You touch one object that is no larger than 2-tiles in any dimension. Until the spell ends, the object sheds bright light in a 4-tile radius and dim light for an additional 4 tiles. The light can be colored as you like. Completely covering the object with something opaque blocks the light. The spell ends if you cast it again or dismiss it as an action.
