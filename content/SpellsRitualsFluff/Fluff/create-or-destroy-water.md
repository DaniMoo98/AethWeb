## Description

### Effects

Create Water

You create up to 10 gallons of clean water within range in an open container. Alternatively, the water falls as rain in a 6 tile cube within range, extinguishing exposed flames in the area.

Destroy Water

You destroy up to 10 gallons of water in an open container within range. Alternatively, you destroy fog in a 6 tile cube within range.
