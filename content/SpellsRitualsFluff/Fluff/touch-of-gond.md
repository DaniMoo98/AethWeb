## Description

### Effects

Choose a metal object no more than ten pounds, that is not being worn or carried, attached to another object or fused with another non-metal material. This spell brings that object to a desired temperature, up to near its melting point. A living creature that touches this object will not experience this change in temperature, and suffer no negative consequences.
