## Description

### Effects

You perform a special religious ceremony that is infused with magic. When you cast the spell, choose one of the following rites, the target of which must be within 10 feet of you throughout the casting. The use of the spell does not supersede the requirement for an Alignment/Deity change ticket.

Atonement. You touch one willing creature who has begun to stray from their faith or alignment, and help guide them towards the correct path.

Dedication. You touch one humanoid who wishes to be dedicated to your god's service, and instill them with the principles of that faith.

Funeral Rite. You touch one corpse, and for the next 7 days, the target can't become undead by any means short of a wish spell.

Wedding. You touch adult humanoids willing to be bonded together in marriage. A creature can benefit from this rite again only if widowed.
