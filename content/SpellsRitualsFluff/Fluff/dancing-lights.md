## Description

### Effects

You create up to four torch-sized lights within range, making them appear as torches, lanterns, or glowing orbs that hover in the air for the duration. You can also combine the four lights into one glowing vaguely humanoid form of Medium size. Whichever form you choose, each light sheds dim light in a 2-tile radius.

You can move the lights up to 12 tiles to a new spot within range. A light must be within 4 tiles of another light created by this spell, and a light winks out if it exceeds the spell's range.
