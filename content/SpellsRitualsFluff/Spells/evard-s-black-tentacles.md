## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 10 tiles of you. Force the target creature to make a DEX saving throw. On a failed save, they take 2d8+Spellcasting modifier points of Bludgeoning damage and are Grappled by the tentacles for a single round, or until the target creature uses the Break Free of Tentacles action on their turn to remove the Grappled status early, or other spell or ability to break the grapple. On a successful save, the target takes half damage.

This spell is affected by DT.
