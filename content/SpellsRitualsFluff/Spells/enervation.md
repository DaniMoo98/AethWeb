## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 6 tiles of you. Force the target creature to roll a DEX saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier. On a failed save, the creature takes 3d8+Spellcasting Modifier Necrotic damage, and the caster heals for 1d10 hit points. On a successful save, the target takes half damage.
