## Description

### Effects

The target makes a STR saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier to resist any of Telekinesis' effects. If the target creature fails it's saving throw, they take 2d10+Caster modifier Force damage and are moved 1 tile where the caster chooses.
