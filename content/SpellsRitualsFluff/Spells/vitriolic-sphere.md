## Description

### Effects

As an Action, select 1 target within 8 tiles of you. Force them and all targets within 2 tiles of the target to make a DEX saving throw. On a failed save, they each take 2d8+Spellcasting Modifier Acid damage and suffer -2 DT for 2 turns. Half damage taken and -1 DT for 1 turn on a successful save.
