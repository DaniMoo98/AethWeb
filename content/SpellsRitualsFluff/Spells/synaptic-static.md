## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 12 tiles of you. Force the target creature, and every creature within a 2 tile radius of the target creature, to roll a INT saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier. On a failed save, the target creature takes 3d8+Caster Modifier psychic damage and suffers -2 to their WIS saving throws for 3 turns.
