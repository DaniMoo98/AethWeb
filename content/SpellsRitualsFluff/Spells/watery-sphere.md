## Description

### Effects

Select up to three targets within 4 tiles of you, make them roll a STR Saving throw and are pushed one tile in any direction from the target center. On a failed save, they suffer 2d8 + Caster Modifier Cold Damage, gaining the "Wet" status for 3 rounds.
