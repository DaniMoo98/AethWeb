## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 10 tiles of you. Force the target to make a DEX saving throw. On a failed save, they are pushed 2 tiles in the direction of the caster's choosing as well as take 2d8+Spellcasting Modifier points of Bludgeoning damage. On a successful save, they take half damage.

This spell is affected by DT.
