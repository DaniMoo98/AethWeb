## Description

### Effects

The Caster and every creature around it within a 2 Tile radius, counts as being within Sunlight. You gain the Daylight Status for 3 Rounds.

This spell cannot be used as a pre-buff.

While the Caster has the Daylight Status, they may spend a reaction to use Shine Daylight to effect all creatures within 2 tiles of them. Shine Daylight will also dispel the Cloaked status.

Shadow Step, Cloak of Shadows, and Mantle of Shadows cannot be used within 2 tiles of the Caster while Daylight is active.

Creatures that state they are weak to sunlight in their race page will take 1d8+Spellcasting Modifier Radiant damage immediately.
