## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 6 tiles of you. The caster takes 2d8 points of True damage and the target heals for 2d8+Spellcasting Modifier Hit Points. This spell can revive a downed creature in combat.

If a creature is revived in this way, they take their turn right after the caster that revived them. This ability is usable once per 3 hours
