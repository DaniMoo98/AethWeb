## Description

### Effects

As an Action, select 1 target within 12 tiles. Force them to make a WIS saving throw. On a failed save, they take 1d8+Mastery Necrotic damage. If they are below 30 hitpoints, they take an additional 1d6 Necrotic damage.

Half damage taken and no secondary effect regardless of hitpoints on a successful save.
