## Description

### Effects

Select an unoccupied space within 2 tiles from you that you can see and teleport to that location.

- This Spell triggers the Mage Slayer feat.

- This spell can be used to escape Grappled and Entangled conditions.

- This does not trigger Attacks of Opportunity.

- This does not allow the caster to travel through walls.

- This does not allow the caster to travel through passages that are too small to accomodate the caster's body.
