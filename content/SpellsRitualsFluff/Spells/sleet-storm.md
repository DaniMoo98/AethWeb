## Description

Effects Select 1 target within 10 tiles. Force them and all targets within 2 tiles to make a DEX saving throw. On a failed save, each target takes 2d6+Mastery Cold damage and are knocked Prone. On a successful save, the target's movement is reduced by -1 for 1 turn.
