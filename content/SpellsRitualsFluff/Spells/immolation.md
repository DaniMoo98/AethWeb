## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 10 Tiles of you. Force the target creature to roll a DEX saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier. On a failed save, the target creature takes 3d8+Spellcasting Modifier fire damage immediately, and 1d8 fire damage at the start of each of its turns for 2 turns. On a successful save, they still take half damage.
