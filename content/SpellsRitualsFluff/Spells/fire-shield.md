## Description

### Effects

Place a fire shield on yourself for 4 turns. When you are struck by a melee attack or melee touch spell, spend a Reaction to force that creature to make a saving throw of WIS vs Spell DC. On a failed saving throw, they take 2d8 + Spellcasting Modifier Fire damage. Half damage on a successful save.
