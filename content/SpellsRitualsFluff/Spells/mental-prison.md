## Description

### Effects

You bind a creature within an illusionary cage that only it can see whether it be a wall of fire, a cage with bars of lightning and so on. On a failed INT Saving THR vs Spell DC, the target takes 3d8 + Spellcasting modifier psychic damage and is entangled for 1 round. On a successful save the target takes half damage and is not entangled. Following a successful entangle attempt from this spell, the target is unable to be Entangled by this spell again for 1 round.
