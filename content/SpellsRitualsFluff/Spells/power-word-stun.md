## Description

### Effects

Select 1 target within 12 tiles. Force them to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, they receive 2d8+Casting Modifier Psychic damage and the Paralyzed status for 1 turn, as well as receive -2 to AC and physical saves for the duration of this effect. On a successful save, the target takes half-damage.
