## Description

Effects Select 1 target within 12 tiles. You gain +1 Attack and apply a Hunter's Mark to the target, lasting the duration of combat or until cancelled. Every attack you make against the target deals an additional 2 True damage. Your Hunter's Mark may be cancelled during your turn using the Recall Hunter's Mark free action. When cancelled in this way, you may elect to apply your Hunter's Mark to another target within 12 tiles as a Reaction and costing 1 Spell Point using the Reapply Hunter's Mark ability, provided it is done before the end of your turn.
