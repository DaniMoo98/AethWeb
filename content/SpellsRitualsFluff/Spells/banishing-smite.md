## Description

### Effects

As a Bonus Action gain the ability to unleash your Smite and force a target to take 2d6+Mastery additional force damage. The target makes a CHA save versus your spell casting modifier; on a failed save they are banished for one turn.

This spell has a 1-turn cooldown.
