## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 15 tiles of you. Force the target creature, and all creatures within a 2 tile radius of the target, to roll a CON saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier. On a failed save, the creature takes 3d8+Caster Modifier necrotic damage. On a successful save, they take half damage.
