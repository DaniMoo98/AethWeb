## Description

### Effects

Force everyone within 1 tile radius from you to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, they take 2d6+Spellcasting modifier points of Thunder damage. On a successful save, they take half damage.

Regardless of the saving throws, you then select an unoccupied space within 2 tiles from you that you can see and you teleport to that location.

- This spell triggers the Mage Slayer feat.

- This does not trigger Attacks of Opportunity.

- This does not allow the caster to travel through walls.
