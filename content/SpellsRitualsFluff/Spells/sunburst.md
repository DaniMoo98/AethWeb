## Description

### Effects

Select up to 2 targets within 12 tiles. Force them each to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, they each take 3d8+Spellcasting Modifier Radiant damage and are Dazed for 1 turn. Half damage and no Dazed on a successful save.

Undead and Fiends take 1d8 more damage.
