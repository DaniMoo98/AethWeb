## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 1 tile of you. Make a melee spell attack with the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier against the target creature's AC. You get a +4 to hit. On a successful hit, the target creature takes 2d10+Spell Casting Modifier necrotic damage and the Caster is healed for 1d8+Mastery hitpoints. The target is also Blighted for 1 turn.
