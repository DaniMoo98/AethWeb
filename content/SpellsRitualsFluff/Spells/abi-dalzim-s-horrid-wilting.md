## Description

### Effects

Select 1 target within 12 tiles. Force the target and all others within 3 tiles to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, each target takes 3d8+Spellcasting Modifier Necrotic damage and receives -2 CON saving throws from 2 turns. Half damage and -1 CON saving throws taken on a successful save.
