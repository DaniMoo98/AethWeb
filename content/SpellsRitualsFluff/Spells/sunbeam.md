## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 8 tiles of you. Force the target creature to roll a CON saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier. On a failed save, the target creature takes 3d8+ Caster Modifier radiant damage and is Dazed for 1 Turn. Undead and Fiends take 1d8 more. On a successful save, they still take half damage.
