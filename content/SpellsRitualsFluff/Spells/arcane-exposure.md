## Description

### Effects

Bonus Action: Select 1 target within 12 tiles and make a ranged spell attack against them with +4 to hit. If successful, they take 15% additional damage from all sources of [element] damage for 2 turns. Only one source of Arcane Exposure can be active on a target at one time.

Split into 5 sub-abilities: Acid Cold Fire Lightning Thunder
