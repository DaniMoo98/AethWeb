## Description

### Effects

Select 3 targets within 15 Tiles of you. Force the target creature to make a DEX saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier. On a failed save, the target takes 3d8 + Caster modifier lightning damage.If the target is wearing heavy armor, they suffer -2 to the Saving Throw. On a successful save, they take half damage.
