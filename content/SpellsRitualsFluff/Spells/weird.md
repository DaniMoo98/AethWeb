## Description

### Effects

Select up to 3 targets within 12 tiles. Force them each to make a WIS saving throw. On a failed save, they each take 3d8+Spellcasting Modifier Psychic damage and are Frightened for 2 turns. Half damage and no Frightened status effect on a successful save.
