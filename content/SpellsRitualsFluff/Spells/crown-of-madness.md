## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 10 tiles of you. Force the target to make a WIS saving throw. On a failed save, the target is driven mad and can no longer tell friend from foe until the end of its next turn. The character under the effect of this spell has to do everything in their power to try to kill the character that's closest to them, using only their main action and bonus action. A free action melee/ranged weapon attack must also be used if applicable to the class. This does not include limited charge class abilities.
