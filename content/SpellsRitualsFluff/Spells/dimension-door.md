## Description

### Effects

Select an unoccupied space within 8 tiles from you and teleport to that location.

This spell consumes all of the caster's movement.

- This does not trigger Attacks of Opportunity unless being targeted by the Mage Slayer feat

- The caster does NOT need to see the destination.

- This DOES allow the caster to travel through walls.

- This spell triggers the Mage Slayer feat.
