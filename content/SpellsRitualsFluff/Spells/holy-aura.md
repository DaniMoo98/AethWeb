## Description

### Effects

The caster gains +2 AC and +1 to all saving throws for 3 turns. Creatures of the casters choice within 3 tiles must make a Dex save or take 1d6 Radiant damage. Deals 1d8 more to Fiends and Undead, half damage on a successful save.
