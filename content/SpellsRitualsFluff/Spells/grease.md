## Description

### Effects

Select 1 target within 12 tiles. Force them to make a DEX saving throw. On a failed save, they take 2d8+Spellcasting Modifier Bludgeoning damage. No damage is taken on a successful save. Additionally, force the target and everyone within 2 tiles of them to make a further DEX saving throw. If this save is failed, they are each knocked prone and receive the Greased status for 3 turns, or until they take Fire damage.
