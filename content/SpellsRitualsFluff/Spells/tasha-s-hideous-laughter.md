## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 6 tiles of you. Force the target to make a WIS saving throw. On a failed save, they gain the Prone status and may not use any abilities that target enemy players or include enemy players in their area of effect. This includes intentionally using an AoE attack on yourself to damage the caster of this spell. Receiving damage from the charmer dispels this status.
