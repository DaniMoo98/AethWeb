## Description

Effects Select 3 targets within 12 tiles of you. Each target makes a DEX saving throw. On a failure they take 3d8 + Caster Modifier fire damage, and creatures within 1 tile of them take 1d8+Caster Modifier fire damage. On a successful save, they still take half damage.
