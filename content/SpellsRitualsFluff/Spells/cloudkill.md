## Description

### Effects

Select 1 target within 12 tiles. Force the target and all others within 3 tiles to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, each target takes 3d8+Spellcasting Modifier Poison damage, receives 1d6 Poison damage per turn for 2 turns, and receives the Blighted status effect for 1 turn. Half damage and no status effects taken on a successful save.
