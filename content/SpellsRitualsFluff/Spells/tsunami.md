## Description

Effects Force all targets within 2 tiles to make a STR saving throw. On a failed save, they each take 2d8+Spellcasting Modifier Bludgeoning damage and are pushed or pulled 2 tiles from the caster. They also receive the Wet status effect for 3 rounds, or until they take Lightning or Cold damage. No damage or status effect taken on a successful save.
