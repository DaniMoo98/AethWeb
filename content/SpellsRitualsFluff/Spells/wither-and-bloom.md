## Description

Effects Select up to 2 targets within 8 tiles. Force them to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, they each become Blighted for 1 turn. They also take an amount of True damage equal to the caster's Mastery level, and their maximum hitpoints are reduced by an amount equal to the caster's Mastery level, lasting until the end of combat or until dispelled via Greater Restoration, Greater Vigor, or Balm of the Summer Court.
