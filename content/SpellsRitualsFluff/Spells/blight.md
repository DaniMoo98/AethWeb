## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 10 tiles of you. Force the target to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, they take 2d6+Spellcasting Modifier points of Necrotic damage, the target cannot be targeted by healing spells until the end of their next turn. On a successful save, they take half damage.
