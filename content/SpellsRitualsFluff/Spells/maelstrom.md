## Description

### Effects

Select 1 target within 6 tiles. Force them and all targets within 2 tiles of them to make a STR saving throw. On a failed save, they each take 2d8+Mod Bludgeoning damage and are pulled to the location of the target, as closely as physically possible. Half damage and no forced movement on a successful save.
