## Description

### Effects

Select an unoccupied space within 2 tiles from you that you can see. Teleport to that location. Then, you count as 'Banished' until the start of your next turn. This spell has a cooldown of 1 round.

Note: The Banished status will not fall off until End Turn is used.

- This does not trigger Attacks of Opportunity.

- This does not allow the caster to travel through walls.

- This does not allow the caster to travel through passages that are too small to accomodate the caster's body.
