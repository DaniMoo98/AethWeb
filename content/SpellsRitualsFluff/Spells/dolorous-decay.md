## Description

### Effects

Select 1 target within 6 tiles. Force them to make a DEX saving throw. On a failed save, the target receives the Slowed status for 2 turns, reducing their movement by -2 tiles and removing their Reaction. The target also suffers 1d8 Necrotic damage for 3 turns.

No status effects taken on a successful save.
