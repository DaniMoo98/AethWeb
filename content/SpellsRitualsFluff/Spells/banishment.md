## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 10 tiles of you. Force the target make a CHA saving throw. On a failed save, they are temporarily teleported to a different plane of existence until the end of their next turn.

The Banished creature cannot take any movement, cannot target any other players with spells or abilities or interact with them in any way, and can't be the target of any attacks or spells or be interacted with. They may change weapons or use spells or abilities which only effect themselves during this time.
