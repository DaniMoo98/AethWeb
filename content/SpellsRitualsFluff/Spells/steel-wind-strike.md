## Description

### Effects

Select up to 3 targets within 6 tiles and make a melee spell attack against their AC with +4 to Hit. If successful, deal 3d8+Spellcasting Modifier Force damage, and the caster teleports next to the last target. No damage taken on a successful save.

This spell triggers the Mage Slayer feat.
