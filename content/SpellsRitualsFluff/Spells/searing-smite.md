## Description

### Effects

Spend a bonus action for a target to take 2d6+Mastery additional fire damage. The target makes a DEX save versus your casting modifier; on a failed save they take 1d6 additional fire damage each round for 2 rounds.

This spell has a 1-turn cooldown.
