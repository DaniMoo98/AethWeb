## Description

### Effects

The caster uses their WIS attribute to roll to hit with melee weapons that deal Bludgeoning damage, rather than STR or DEX. Additionally, damage rolls have the caster add their WIS attribute to the damage rolls as opposed to STR or DEX.

This effect lasts until the caster's next rest.
