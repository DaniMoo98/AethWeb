## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 8 Tiles of you. Force the target creature to make a DEX saving throw versus the Caster's Spellcasting Modifier. On a failed save, the target takes 4d8+Spellcasting Modifier force damage and has their AC lowered by -2 for 2 rounds. On a successful save, they take half damage.

Targets brought to 0 health cannot be revived after being downed by this spell.
