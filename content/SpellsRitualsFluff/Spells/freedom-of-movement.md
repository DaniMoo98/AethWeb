## Description

### Effects

Select a creature within 4 tiles of yourself. That creature loses Entangled, Grappled, and Paralyzed statuses, as well as gaining the ability to escape non-magical restraints immediately. Grants immunity to Entangled and Grappling effects for 2 rounds.
