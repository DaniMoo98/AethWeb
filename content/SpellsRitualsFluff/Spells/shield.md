| **Material Components** | Verbal, Somatic |
| **Category** | Novice in Spells & Rituals |

## Description

Effects The caster gains +2 DT for 2 turns. While the creature is effected by the Shield spell, they may not be the target of Magic Missile. A creature under the effects of this spell will lose it if they become Silenced or lose Verbal components.
