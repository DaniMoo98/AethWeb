## Description

### Effects

Select a target within 8 tiles of you. The target and every creature within a 1 tile radius from the target are forced to make a DEX saving throw. On a failed save, they also take 1d12+Spellcasting Modifier points of Cold damage and 1d12 points of Bludgeoning damage. All targets movement is reduced by 2 for 1 round. On a successful save, they take half damage.

This spell is affected by DT.
