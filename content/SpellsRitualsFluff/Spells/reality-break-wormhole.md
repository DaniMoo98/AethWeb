## Description

### Effects

Select 1 target within 6 tiles. Force them to make a WIS saving throw. On a failed save, they take 3d8+Casting Modifier Force damage, are moved 2 tiles to a visible location of the caster's choosing, and are knocked Prone. Half damage, 1 tile of forced movement, and no Prone status effect on a successful save.
