## Description

### Effects

Each object in a 20-foot cube within range is outlined in blue, green, or violet light (your choice). Select one target when the spell is cast that is also outlined in light if it fails a DEX Saving THR versus your Spell DC.

On a failed saving throw, they suffer -2 AC for 1 turn.

This spell has the Illuminated effect, preventing the use of Cloak of Shadows, Mantle of Shadows, and Shadow Step within its range while active.
