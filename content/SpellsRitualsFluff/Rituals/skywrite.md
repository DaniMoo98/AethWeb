## Description

Effects No character specific information can be spread without the implicit consent of involved players. You cause up to ten words to form in a part of the sky you can see. The words appear to be made of cloud and remain in place for a short duration. The words dissipate when the spell ends. The message broadcast by the spell will appear in the rumors channel for viewing.
