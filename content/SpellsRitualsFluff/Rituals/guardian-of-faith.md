## Description

### Effects

A Large spectral guardian appears and hovers for the duration in an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within range. The space must be within your clan's owned lands. The guardian occupies a 1x1 tile area and is indistinct except for a gleaming sword and shield emblazoned with the symbol of your deity. The guardian does not prevent movement through its space.

To you and any creatures you designate when you cast this spell, the guardian is harmless. Any other creature that attempts to pass through the tile occupied by the guardian must make a Dexterity saving throw against your Spell Save DC. On a failed save, the creature takes Medium Wounds. On a success the creature is unharmed. Regardless of whether the creature succeeds or fails, the spell ends.

Attempting to pass by a Guardian of Faith that you are not designated harmless to is considered infiltration. The appropriate Infiltration or Raid ticket (if 5 or more players are involved) must be opened in accordance with those rulesets.

A Theft and Trespass POI must be left behind if a Guardian of Faith has been triggered.

Only one Glyph of Warding or Guardian of Faith may be within a clan's owned lands at any one time.
