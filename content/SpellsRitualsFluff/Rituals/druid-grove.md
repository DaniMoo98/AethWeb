## Description

### Effects

You invoke the spirits of nature to protect an area that is personally cultivated by the druid. The area can be as small as 6 tiles wide or as large as 18 tiles wide. This may not be placed inside of artificial structures, but can be placed on top of them or within ruins that have been overtaken by greenery.

Once the ritual is complete, the druid gains the benefits of the Ritual Specialization feat when casting ritual spells in their grove. At Master, any druid casting a ritual in the grove gains the feat's benefits.

Gain +1 to the DC of ritual spells, and advantage on skill rolls and spellcasting checks which take place as part of a ritual.
