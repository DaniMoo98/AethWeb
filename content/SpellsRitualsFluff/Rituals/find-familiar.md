## Description

### Effects

You gain the service of a familiar, a spirit that takes an animal form you choose: a raven, an owl or a crow or another Ravencrest bird. Other tiny beasts may be submitted for approval in the ritual ticket, and Wizards may submit a tiny elemental Tot as well.

Your familiar acts independently of you, but it always obeys your commands.Your familiar can understand you, but it cannot speak. Your familiar can perform simple tasks, such as fetching tiny items, but cannot perform actions such as eavesdropping or infiltration. If you are ever more than 4 tiles from your familiar, it disappears harmlessly into a pocket dimension.

If your familiar is ever harmed, it disappears, leaving behind no physical form. It reappears after you cast this spell again. As an action, you can temporarily dismiss the familiar to a pocket dimension. Alternatively, you can dismiss it forever. As an action while it is temporarily dismissed, you can cause it to reappear in any unoccupied space within 4 tiles of you.

You can't have more than one familiar at a time. If you cast this spell while you already have a familiar, you instead cause it to adopt a new form. Choose one of the forms from the above list. Your familiar transforms into the chosen creature.
