## Description

### Effects

Shroud your passage from sight, making your trail harder to follow.

You may cast this spell any time you must place a trackable POI (see: Tracking and POIs) such as after combat, or during infiltrations. Any POIs you place for that encounter will instead obey the Magic POI rules for tracking, requiring the use of Knowledge: Arcana in place of Survival.

In addition, you may add your Spellcasting Modifier to the tracking DC, rather than half of your Survival or Knowledge: Arcana.
