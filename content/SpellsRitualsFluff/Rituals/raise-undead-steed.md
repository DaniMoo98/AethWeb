## Description

### Effects

Through the sacrifice of magical power (and a good bit of gold), one attuned to the forces of death and undeath can summon a steed of unliving horror. After your ritual ticket for casting this ritual is closed out, you may submit the transcript and the decay log in an item request ticket and a staff member will spawn you your chosen mount.
