## Description

### Effects

You touch a creature and stimulate its natural healing ability.

The target's severed body members (fingers, legs, tails, and so on), if any, are regrown after 1 hour. If you have the severed part and hold it to the stump, the spell instantaneously causes the limb to knit to the stump.
