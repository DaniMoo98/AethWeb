## Description

### Effects

You gain the ability to enter a tree and move from inside it to inside another tree of the same kind within 100 tiles. Both trees must be living and at least the same size as you.

You instantly know the location of all other trees of the same kind within 100 tiles and, as part of the move used to enter the tree, can either pass into one of those trees or step out of the tree you're in.

You appear in a spot of your choice within 1 tile of the destination tree.
