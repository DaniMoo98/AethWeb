## Description

Effects You touch a dead humanoid or a piece of a dead humanoid. Provided that the creature has been dead no longer than 10 days, the spell forms a new adult body for it and then calls the soul to enter that body. If the target's soul isn't free or willing to do Player characters may only be reincarnated once per character per season. Exceptions may be made if a player wishes to make use of reincarnate for plot reasons, rather than in response to a PK but the request must be made to moderation prior to the attempt at the ritual.
