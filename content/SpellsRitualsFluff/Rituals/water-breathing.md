## Description

### Effects

This spell grants up to ten willing creatures you can see within 4 tiles the ability to breathe underwater until the spell ends. Affected creatures also retain their normal mode of respiration.

Although this spell grants water breathing, it does not grant the ability to use swimming as a means of fleeing combat.
