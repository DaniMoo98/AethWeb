## Description

### Effects

You set an alarm against unwanted intrusion. Choose a door, a window, or an area within range that is no larger than 3x3 tiles. Until the spell ends, an alarm alerts you whenever a Tiny or larger creature touches or enters the warded area.

When you cast the spell, you can designate creatures that won't set off the alarm. You also choose whether the alarm is mental or audible.

A mental alarm alerts you with a ping in your mind if you are within 1 mile of the warded area. This ping awakens you if you are sleeping. This is represented by having your discord I.D. attached to the poi, if during infiltration of any kind the alarm is triggered the infiltrator is required to post in very far an emote of them triggering the alarm bell, and staff will directly inform the owner of the alarm spell.

This ritual is persistent until dispelled. Only three Alarm spells may be active in any clan base, regardless of number of casters.
