## Description

### Effects

You place a magical command on a creature that you can see within range, forcing it to carry out some service or refrain from some action or course of activity as you decide. If the creature can understand you, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or obey the command. While the creature is affected by the spell, it takes Medium Wounds each time it acts in a manner directly counter to your instructions, but no more than once each day. A creature that can't understand you is unaffected by the spell.

You can issue any command you choose, short of an activity that would result in certain death. Should you issue a suicidal command, the spell ends. You also cannot compel or be compelled to conduct an action that would constitute NVL.

You can end the spell early by using an action to dismiss it. A remove curse spell also ends it.

Geas, like curses, requires consent from the targeted player.
