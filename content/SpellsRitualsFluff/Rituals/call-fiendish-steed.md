## Description

### Effects

Through fiendish bargains and treading the veil between planes, one can call a steed of Hellish or Abyssal origins to their side. Horses descended from true Nightmares, purported to give their owners troubled sleep at night.

After your ritual ticket for casting this ritual is closed out, you may submit the transcript and gold decay log in an item request ticket and a staff member will spawn you your chosen mount.
