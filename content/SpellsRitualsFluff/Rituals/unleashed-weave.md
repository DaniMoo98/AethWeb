## Description

### Effects

By harnessing the power of your inner will, your power is made manifest in a new and vibrant manner you can call upon.

This is a cosmetic feature, and does not carry any other mechanical or combat interactions. Once your ritual ticket for casting this ritual has been closed out, you may submit the transcript and a screenshot of the decay log in an item request ticket, and staff will spawn you your chosen aura.
