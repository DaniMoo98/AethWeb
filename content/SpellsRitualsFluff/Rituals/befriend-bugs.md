## Description

### Effects

Through druidic knowledge and ancient rites, you have forged a friendship with a cloud of insects, and they will now follow you where you go.

This is a cosmetic feature and not equivalent to the Swarmkeeper swarm feature and the insects cannot act like a Mage Hand in the same way, defend you, or attack anyone. Once your ritual ticket for casting this ritual has been closed out, you may submit the transcript and a screenshot of the decay log in an item request ticket, and staff will spawn you your chosen aura
