## Description

### Effects

You touch a corpse or other remains. For a day, the target is protected from decay and can't become undead.

If the caster is an Expert full caster or Master hybrid caster, this can be extended to a week by doubling the cost.

The spell also effectively extends the time limit on raising the target from the dead, since days spent under the influence of this spell don't count against the time limit of spells such as raise dead.
