## Description

### Effects

At your touch, attempt to remove all curses affecting one creature or object end -- the first curse is removed at base cost, with any extra curses taking an additional small component per curse. Roll 1d20+Spellcasting Modifier aganist the caster's Spell DC. On a success, the curse is removed. On a failure, the curse is not removed, but the components are still spent.

If the object is a cursed magic item from a DM event, its curse remains, but the spell breaks its owner's attunement to the object so it can be removed or discarded. This cannot remove curses by gods or powerful outsiders.
