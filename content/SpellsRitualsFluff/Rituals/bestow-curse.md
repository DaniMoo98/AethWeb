## Description

### Effects

You touch a creature, and that creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become cursed for the duration of the spell.

An item may also be cursed, delivering the same effect when touched by an individual other than the caster. When cast upon on an object, a

magical tracking POI

must be placed and a screenshot of its description and placement submitted within the ritual ticket.

When you cast this spell, choose the nature of the curse from the following options on the

Curses

Page. A remove curse spell ends this effect. A curse can only be bestowed on the same target by the caster once per week.

This ritual is persistent until dispelled or triggered.

OOC consent is

required

before casting.
