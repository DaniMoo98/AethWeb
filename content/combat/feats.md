# FEATS

Feats are a way to further customize your character, granting them unique abilities or bonuses.

### ANATOMIST
*Requirement: Intelligence 1 or Wisdom 1*
You have studied the anatomy of many creatures, granting you the following benefits:
- You gain the **Surgery** ability, allowing you to treat wounds.
- Your **Medicine** skill increases by +1.

### ALERT
You are always on your guard, granting you the following benefits:
- You cannot be surprised while you are conscious.
- Other creatures don't gain advantage on attack rolls against you as a result of being unseen by you.

### DUAL WIELDING
*Requirement: Dexterity 1*
You master fighting with two weapons, gaining the following benefits:
- You gain a +1 bonus to AC while you are wielding a separate melee weapon in each hand.
- You can use two-weapon fighting even when the one-handed melee weapons you are wielding aren't light.
- You can draw or stow two one-handed melee weapons when you would normally be able to draw or stow only one.

### MIRACLE WORKER
*Requirement: Ability to cast spells*
You have a gift for healing, granting you the following benefits:
- You gain the **Magical Healing** ability.
- When you roll dice to restore hit points with a spell, you can treat any roll of a 1 on a die as a 2.
