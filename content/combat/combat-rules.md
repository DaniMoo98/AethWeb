# COMBAT RULES

To mitigate the detrimental effect large-scale open world PVP can have, the maximum PVP size allowable has been set to a limit of **5 players on any one 'side'** for a total allowable maximum of **5v5**. 5v5 may only be exceeded in a raid, up to 8v8, on the event server.

Fights may have more than two 'sides', provided that the total number of players does not exceed **10**. Use of this rule to circumvent the 5-per-side limit is considered exploitation.

When going to an LFRP with an advertised number where combat may occur, you must meet the number and not exceed or outnumber it. You may outnumber with express OOC consent obtained **before** you approach.

---

## STANDARDS

### Hit Points (HP)

Each player character starts with **30 Hit Points (HP)**. Every point invested into **Constitution** enhances HP by **6 points**. The class determines additional HP gained per mastery level.

### Spell Points (SP)

Every player starts with **4 Spell Points (SP)**. Each attribute point in the **Spellcasting attribute** grants an additional **4 Spell Points**.

### Actions

Each character has: **4 tiles of Movement**, **1 Action**, **1 Bonus Action**, and **1 Reaction**. Free actions may be available depending on class or race.

- Unless otherwise permitted, a creature may not cast more than **one Action spell per round**. Bonus Action/Reaction spells don't impede Actions.

### Spell DC

**Spell DC** = Spellcasting Modifier + 10. This represents the required saving throw roll for most spells.

### Armor Class

Starting AC is **10**. Each level in **Dexterity** grants **+1 AC** (capped by armor type). Each Dexterity point also grants **+1 initiative**.

### Movement

Starting speed is determined by **race** (each tile = 5 feet). Dash grants an additional 4 tiles. Movement can be split between actions and bonus actions.

- Creatures must move in straight lines while tracking; change direction by re-pressing the track button
- Creatures cannot pass through each other during combat unless they have the Small racial perk
- Doorway adjustments (changing ToT shape) incur **-1 movement** on that turn
- Teleportation abilities do not incur the doorway movement penalty

**Teleportation:** Requires line of sight to the destination surface (unless noted otherwise). You cannot end a teleport in a climbing position.

**Line of Sight:** Targeting a creature behind substantial cover is impermissible. You must see at least **50%** of a target without obstruction. Players, pets, and mounts do not obstruct line of sight.

**Attack of Opportunity:** Triggered when a hostile creature moves beyond melee range without Disengaging, or uses a ranged weapon within melee range. One AoO per round using your Reaction. Spell attacks and teleportation do **not** provoke AoO. Forced movement does **not** trigger AoO.

## PLAYER VERSUS PLAYER (PVP) COMBAT

### PVP Intent

Requirements for declaring PVP:
- Target is not already in ongoing PVP
- **One round of posts minimum** before declaring intent (two preferred)
- You may **not** move in the same emote as the declaration

Declare PVP intent using **/pvp Intent** (Shout range minimum). Responses:
- **/PVP Join** — joining the combat
- **/PVP Flee** — intending to flee (initiative is rolled, then flee action replaces pre-buff)
- **/PVP Observe** — present but not participating in combat

### PVP Intent & Places of Interest

No PvP within **20 tiles** of a Gathering Zone or Warp. PVP may be initiated at a Gathering Zone, but both parties must move 20 tiles away. **No PVP can be initiated at warps.**

### Equipment

Players may start combat with any combination of owned martial weapons equipped. Equipping/unequipping armor in response to a PVP intention is **prohibited**.

### Pre-Combat Buff Round

Each player may cast a **single buff spell** targeting self or allies. Being ambushed prevents casting any buffs.

Eligible pre-buffs:
- Magical Buffs, Barbarian Rages (Expert), Potions

**NOT** eligible:
- Hostile actions, equipping armor runes, movement (unless class/race allows)

### Scene Lock

When PVP intent is dropped, a scene lock begins and continues through combat. After combat: **4 rounds of emotes** for winning/losing teams to exit undisturbed.

- Departing during these 4 rounds = protected by scene lock
- Passersby must leave the area unless given explicit permission from all parties
- Once a participant departs, they may not return nor send reinforcements for **3 hours**

### Fight Radius Rule

All players must remain within **25 tiles** of the fight starting point and/or the nearest downed player.

### Observing PVP

- PVP Observers may witness but **not** interfere with initiative or combat
- Observers cannot interfere with consequences or repeatedly declare intent
- Characters who arrive during scene lock must **leave the area completely**

### Tracking After PVP

All parties involved must leave a POI after battle. See **Tracking & Theft** for full rules.

### Downed Players

Downed players cannot be directly targeted with abilities. AOE spells may hit a downed player as part of their radius, but cannot be targeted directly at them.

## UNFAVORABLE ODDS (SKIRMISH RULE)

Skirmish consequences result from winners outnumbering victims by **2 or more** (3v1, 4v2, 5v3). See **Consequence Rules** for full details.

/PVP Observe players count toward the ratio for calculating consequences.

## ACTIONS, BONUS ACTIONS, & MOVEMENT

### Actions

| Action | Description |
|---|---|
| **Attack** | Attack with ranged/melee weapon or spell. Damage type auto-adjusts for resistances. |
| **Shove** | Push target within 1 tile. Opposed STR/DEX save — on success, they're Prone. |
| **Grapple** | Requires no off-hand weapon/shield. Roll 10 + STR vs target's STR/DEX save. |
| **Disarm** | While grappling: target makes STR save vs DC 10 + your STR. |
| **Disengage** | Prevents AoO when withdrawing. Not available while mounted. |
| **Dash** | +4 tiles of movement. Available while mounted. |
| **Switch/Pickup Weapon** | Remove Disarmed status or equip a Rune. |
| **Mount** | Climb onto a mount. No further movement after this action. |
| **Flee** | Requires no enemies within 4 tiles. Enemies within 14 tiles may Chase (contested roll). |

**Forced Auto-Flee:** Moving 16+ tiles from nearest target = automatic flee and Exit Combat. Cannot be forced via forced movement abilities.

### Bonus Actions

**Offhand Attack:** Additional attack with Light weapon in off-hand, at **-2 penalty** to hit. No damage/hit bonuses from class features or spells unless using the Dual Wielding fighting style.

### Reactions

Used on your turn unless a specific trigger allows off-turn use. See spell descriptions for trigger conditions.

### Movement Actions

- **Get Up:** Free Action + 2 tiles of movement to remove Prone
- **Climb:** Costs half of creature's speed. Some classes allow full-speed climbing.

## WEAPON & ARMOR RULES

- Switching weapons requires the Switch Weapon/Pickup action
- Versatile weapons: switching grip requires the Switch action
- Off-hand attacks with Light weapons: -2 penalty, no STR/DEX bonus unless Dual Wielding style

### Weapon Feats

| Feat | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Defensive** | +1 AC |
| **Versatile** | One-handed or two-handed use |
| **Disarm** | Opposed Athletics/Sleight of Hand within 3 tiles |
| **Entangle** | Immobilize target within 8 tiles for 1 round |
| **Thrown** | Throw at target up to 5 tiles |
| **Breach** | +3 breach skill for 5 minutes |
| **Cavalier** | +2 to hit while mounted |
| **Keen** | +1 to attack rolls |
| **Finesse** | Use DEX or STR for hit/damage |
| **Light** | Off-hand bonus action attack at -2 |

## MOUNTED COMBAT

Characters below **250cm** can mount, granting **6 tiles of movement** regardless of race/class. Not increased by temporary movement buffs.

While mounted, you can: Cast Spells, Attack, Switch Weapons, Offhand Attack, Flee, Dash, Mount.

### Mounting Mid-Combat

Use the Mount Up action, spending 2 Movement. Must be able to mechanically interact with the mount.

### Dismounting

Methods: Shove, Prone-applying ability, teleportation (except Dimension Door), being Grappled while riding.

### Forced Movement While Riding

STR or DEX save (whichever is higher) against **DC 17**. On failure: dismounted, Prone. Success: forced movement but stay mounted.

## FLIGHT, SWIM, & CLIMBING

### Flight

Flying creatures targeted by PVP must land and engage or immediately attempt to flee. Three forms of flight:
- Fly spell (6 SP + 1 Action)
- Racial Flying (1 Action)
- Wildshape Flying (1 Wildshape charge + 1 Action)

Flying in combat: full-speed terrain navigation, full-speed climbing (can't end in air/on wall), access to Flying Flee.

**Fleeing While Flying:** Countered by Bola/Tanglefoot throw, entangle spell, grappling, or another flier making a contested Chase roll.

### Swimming

Subject to Aquatic Combat rules. See the Aquatic Combat rules page.

### Climbing

All creatures climb at **double movement cost** unless class/race specifies otherwise. No spells with somatic components or combat abilities while climbing (unless Spider Climb or similar).

Falling while climbing: Entangled, Prone, Grappled, Paralyzed, or forced movement = immediate fall.

### Falling

- **4+ tiles onto dry land/shallow water:** Removed from combat + Wounds
- **6+ tiles into deep water (Swimming status):** Removed from combat + Wounds
- Intentional fall of 2+ tiles consumes all remaining movement

## SPELLS

### Spells Outside of Combat

- Casting outside combat is purely roleplayed; no intent declaration required
- Negative effects on another player may be considered PvP intent
- Consent required for negative spells on other players

### Magical Charm & Dominate

See the **Charm & Dominate Rules** page. In combat, follow the status table and spell page rules.

### AC Increasing Spells

- **Magical Armor** (Mage Armor, Stoneskin): real-world time duration, can be cast inside or outside combat, cannot be stacked
- **Magical Shield** (Shield, Blur, Shield of Faith): round-based duration, combat only, cannot be stacked, 1-round cooldown after expiry

### Weapon Enchantments

Can be stacked, as they are always self-targeted.

### Debuff Spells

Cannot be cast on allies to prevent enemy debuffs. Strategic CC on allies (e.g., Banishment) is allowed.

### Downed Status & Healing

Healing spells do **not** revive a creature at 0 HP. Only spells explicitly designed for revival may do so. A creature can only be revived **once per combat** unless the ability specifies otherwise.

## STEALTH

### Discovery

When a stealthed creature is discovered via Perception check, they are no longer exempt from PVP intent. Discovery eliminates the possibility of a surprise round.

### Stealth & Combat

A stealthed creature targeted by PVP intent may choose to exclude themselves. If they engage, they must reveal themselves and remove stealth status. Creatures cannot join PVP once initiative is established.

### Stealth to Avoid Encounters

Requires adequate cover/concealment when approached. Roll Stealth vs the searching creature's Perception.

### Stealth & Surprise

When a stealthed creature initiates PVP and passes the Stealth vs Perception roll, they **always act first** regardless of initiative. Non-stealthed targets cannot:
- Apply buffs
- Consume buff consumables
- Equip/unequip weapons or armor

Players with the **Alert** feat cannot be surprised (only the feat-holder in a group).

Each character receives the following starting equipment:

**Main Hand:** Dull Dagger, Flimsy Shortbow, Wooden Mace, Rusted Short Sword, or Frying Pan

**Offhand:** Dull Dagger, Rusted Short Sword, Frying Pan, or Wooden Shield

**Armor:** Ragged Armor or Rusty Ringmail

**Currency:** 5 silver pieces
