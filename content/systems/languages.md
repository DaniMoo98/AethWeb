# LANGUAGES

Crossroads separates language use into two progression tracks.

## Language Tiers

| Tier | Notes |
|---|---|
| Common Languages | Baseline language group used across most daily roleplay. |
| Exotic Languages | Higher-investment language group for specialized backgrounds and rare cultures. |

## Learning Languages

Languages are typically gained through approved progression methods (class features, mastery perks, and staff-approved roleplay pathways).

- Common language unlocks are generally lower-cost than exotic unlocks.
- Exotic language unlocks usually require stronger narrative justification.
- Keep all language additions documented in your character records and relevant ticket where required.
