# MAGIC

Magic on Crossroads is organized by spell mastery rank and ritual/fluff utility categories.

## Spell Mastery Tracks

| Track | Registry |
|---|---|
| Cantrips / Novice / Adept / Expert / Master Spells | [Spell Registry](/page?p=SpellsRitualsFluff/Spells/Spells) |
| Ritual Magic | [Ritual Registry](/page?p=SpellsRitualsFluff/Rituals/Rituals) |
| Fluff / Utility Magic | [Fluff Registry](/page?p=SpellsRitualsFluff/Fluff/Fluff) |

## Practical Use

- Your class and mastery rank determine what you can access.
- Spell and ritual availability should always match your current class features and approved sheet state.
- Progression into higher tiers is tied to mastery progression.
