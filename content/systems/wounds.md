# WOUNDS SYSTEM

The wounds system tracks injury severity and recovery pacing in roleplay and mechanics.

## Core Rule Areas

- How the wounds system works
- Representing wounds in roleplay
- Dealing wounds as consequences
- Healing wounds
- After-treatment handling
- Regeneration portrayal
- Treatment cooldowns

## Wound Stages

| Stage | Recently Injured Timer |
|---|---|
| Lesser Wounds | 1 hour |
| Medium Wounds | 2 hours |
| Serious Wounds | 3 hours |
| Devastating Wounds | 4 hours |

## Healing Paths

- Natural healing
- First aid
- Surgery
- Magical healing
- Rest-based recovery
