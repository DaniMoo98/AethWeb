Divine magic is the power of the gods made manifest in the world. Clerics are conduits for that power, chosen to fulfill a calling greater than themselves. They combine healing, support, and battlefield control with enough martial training to stand in the front line when their faith demands it.

Every creature has a starting amount of Spell Points (SP) equal to 4.

Each attribute point spent during character creation towards a caster's Spellcasting Attribute increases their Spell Points (SP) by another 4.

Choose from 6 domains, each one shaping how your faith expresses itself in play.

Clerics can come from any land or station in life, but they must serve a deity active in 5E. Most maintain some connection to temples, priesthoods, or divine orders, and many adventure because their god demands it of them.

Please take care when choosing your cleric's deity. If you are unsure which god best suits your character, open a lore-inquiry ticket before whitelisting.

Changing deity as a cleric requires a minimum of 3 sessions of 1 hour each of roleplay. For long-term story progression involving divine change, open a lore-inquiry ticket.

## Domains & Subdomains

### Base Domains

| Domain | Focus |
|:---|:---|
| **Death** | Necrotic magic, undeath, decay, and the underworld |
| **Life** | Pure healing, vitality, and preserving allies |
| **Nature** | Wilderness, elemental balance, and druidic overlap |
| **Trickery** | Illusions, deception, stealth, and disruption |
| **Twilight** | Protection, darkness as comfort, and vigilance |
| **War** | Martial support, radiant offense, and battlefield leadership |

### Adept Subdomain Table

At **Adept**, clerics may open a ticket to select **one subdomain**. That subdomain must be in line with the domains of their deity.

| Subdomain | Benefits |
|:---|:---|

Gain **+6 SP** and access to the fluff spells **Control Flames, Dancing Lights, Gust, Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, Mold Earth, Prestidigitation, Shape Water**. |
| **Death** | Gain **+4 HP**, **Necrotic Resistance**, **Ray of Enfeeblement**, and access to the expert ritual **Antilife Shell**. |
| **Forge** | Gain **Fire Resistance**, **+1 AC from armor**, **+1 hit with weapons**, and access to the fluff spell **Touch of Gond**. |
| **Grave** | Gain **+6 HP**, **Circle of Mortality**, and access to the expert ritual **Antilife Shell**. Circle of Mortality: at the start of your first turn after an ally within 5 tiles drops to 0 HP, you may use an Action to heal them for **2d6 + casting modifier**, remove Downed, and let them immediately take their turn. Once per short rest, and it cannot affect a target already restored by a similar ability in the same combat. |
| **Knowledge** | Gain either the **Genius** or **Linguist** feat, access to **Comprehend Languages** and **Identify**, **+6 SP**, and **+1 INT and WIS saves**. |

| **Light** | Learn **Fireball** and **Scorching Ray**. Gain **+2 damage** on radiant and fire spells of expert rank or lower, including relevant damage-over-time effects. |

| **Peace** | Gain **+1 on all WIS and CHA skills** and **Protective Bond**. Select 1 target within 10 tiles, move 2 tiles toward them, and heal them for **1d4 + Mastery**. This can be used while grappled, frees you from grapples, and can be used twice per rest. |
| **Tempest** | Learn **Chain Lightning** and **Ice Storm** and gain **Lightning Resistance**. |

<div class="details-content">

Create a perfect illusion of yourself for the duration of combat, granting **+1 AC** and access to **Clone Confusion** and **Sneak Attack**.

This Channel Divinity may be activated during the pre-buff round and may be dispelled by **Dispel Magic**.

**Clone Confusion**

Spend a Channel Divinity charge and a Bonus Action to select 1 target within 5 tiles. They make a **WIS save**. On a failed save, they suffer **-2 WIS saves** for 2 turns and take **1d8 + Casting Modifier Psychic damage**. Half damage and no save penalty on success.

Creatures using **Witch Sight** can see through the illusion and are immune to Clone Confusion.

**Sneak Attack**

While your Channel Divinity is active, you gain access to sneak attack. Following a successful weapon attack, use a free action to deal **3d4 true damage** if another ally is within 1 tile of the target or the target is **Prone, Entangled, Paralyzed, Asleep, Dazed, Grappled, Bleeding, or Slowed**. Once per round.

You may also sneak attack a target successfully affected by **Clone Confusion**.
