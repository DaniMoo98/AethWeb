Civilians are the true heart of civilization. It's those who work the farms, light the forges, and tend the taverns that bring life to Faerun. While heroes and adventurers serve to protect the lands from threats local and otherworldly alike, it is Civilians that grow the world around them by creating the communities that all call home.

Civilians are not magic weilders or exceptional fighters, but they excel in their own ways.

Choose from one of 3 subclasses.

Civilians thrive when working together to overcome the greatest of obstacles.

While leaving the fighting to the professionals, Civilians are crafty and thrive as the lifeblood of the economy.

<summary>**Apothecary**</summary>
<div class="details-content">

Devoted to the study of herbs, alchemy, and remedies, Apothecaries are often found tending shops or traveling with satchels of tinctures and poultices. While not trained for combat, they possess keen knowledge of plants, potions, surgery and natural cures, making them invaluable in tending the sick, brewing tonics, or identifying poisons.
