# MASTERY PROGRESSION

Characters advance through mastery ranks by earning **Mastery Marks** through roleplay and server activity.

## Mastery Levels

| Rank | Description |
|---|---|
| Novice | Early-stage character development, still learning the class identity and baseline mechanics. |
| Adept | Reliable and experienced in the class, with broader practical competence. |
| Expert | Highly capable specialist with developed class mastery and stronger narrative impact. |
| Master | Peak class expression, achieved through marks plus approved mastery application. |

## Ways to Earn Mastery Marks

- Every Other Day Kit: **2 marks every 48 hours** (passive progression).
- DM / Server Events: typically **3-10 marks** based on participation, stakes, and outcomes.
- Quests: selected quests can grant up to **5 marks**.

## Mark Requirements

| Progression Step | Marks Required |
|---|---|
| Novice -> Adept | 10 |
| Adept -> Expert | 25 |
| Expert -> Master Qualification | 115 + approved mastery application |

## Master Qualification

Reaching Master is not an automatic purchase. A character must reach **115 marks** and complete either:

- a **Mastery Through RP** application, or
- a **DM-sponsored Mastery Event** application.

Approval is based on demonstrated class understanding, narrative coherence, and roleplay quality.

## Character Growth (Expert+)

At Expert, marks may also be spent on character growth options via ticketed review.

### Perks

- Retraining: Stats (**40**) or Feats (**40**), or both together (**70**) when eligible.
- Interpreter: learn one language with required RP sessions.
- Expertise: add **+2** to one skill (maximum value **10**, non-stacking).
- Hidden Potential: purchase one eligible trait.
- Minor Arcana: purchase one listed utility spell.

### Perk Costs

| Perk | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Retrain Stats | 40 | One-time mark purchase route. |
| Retrain Feats | 40 | One-time mark purchase route. |
| Retrain Stats + Feats | 70 | Only if not already used separately. |
| Interpreter (Common) | 40 | Requires 3 language-focused RP sessions. |
| Interpreter (Exotic) | 60 | Requires 3 language-focused RP sessions. |
| Expertise | 60 | +2 to a skill, cap 10, does not stack with other expertise sources. |
| Hidden Potential Trait | 60 | Choose one trait only. |
| Minor Arcana | 40 | Choose one spell from the approved list. |
