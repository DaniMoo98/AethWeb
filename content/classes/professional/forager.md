You are connected to nature in a unique way. After spending hours digging and picking through land, you've found yourself becoming familiar with what the gods have to offer. What people negligably walk past and over each and every day, to you, is a golden opportunity and a treasure trove of value. Townships are lucky to have you and those like you. The resources you've become adept in finding are used for various medical, combative and utilitarian ways.

The people around you rely on your unique eye to find and utilize the basic resources of nature that they would otherwise overlook.
