The feeling of contrast between freedom and confinement has become so common for you that it barely phases you at all anymore. Coming back from the depths of the planet is something you've come to expect when it comes time to reflect on the day. You've made a living mining. This implies that you've worked your body hard, extracting any value from the ground you can find. As dangerous and taxing as the work is, it pays big if you strike that one jackpot vein!

Your community relies on workers like you to bring in various materials for use and precious metals for all the riches and status the town can manage.
