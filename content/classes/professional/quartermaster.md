Since the dawn of the 'intelligent' creature, our ancestors have always sought out a way to protect themselves. Smithing, metalworking, and the use of weaponry. You take this to the next level. Metal from the mines, skins from a hunt, you take it all and make something that will take a hit or two when your clients take a hit or ten. Hell, you're the whole reason three or four of these lunatics are still able to sit at the tavern after (for some reason) taking on a stone giant the same day. Being able to craft armor and weapons has a huge payoff, and it's not just so that barmaiden comes back another night to be 'welcomed home'. You've managed to make a living from it, too.

Your fellow town's people rely on you to make functional (and sometimes fashionable) suits of armor for them. It plays a tremendous role in their safety.
