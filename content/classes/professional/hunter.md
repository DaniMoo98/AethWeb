Over and over, you find yourself awaiting the perfect moment. Whether it be sitting in a tree, waiting for a plan to fall into place, or slowly stalking an animal what's soon to become your next meal, or next coin. No matter your tool of choice, you're very familiar with the techniques and traditions around gutting, skinning, cleaning, and of course killing an animal. Most favor prey, though the particularly brave (or perhaps careless) persue predators just like themselves, or even monsters. People have relied on this skill for centuries to not only keep a market running, but to sustain themselves on a more primitive level.

You're the one people can depend on to deliver.
