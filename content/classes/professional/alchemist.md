The various of brews and concoctions made by alchemists can be very interesting and diverse. They, of all things in this world, are what piqued your attention. While potentially tedious or boring to some, you've found the craft to be one where depth and fulfillment can be found. After all, it's extremely productive to craft a potion that can improve brain processes, poison a group of ner-do-gooders or even save a life. Whether you study to find as many recipes as possible or dangerously experiment with your own combinations, you've managed to pay the bills by producing potions that others request of you.

Those around you come to you to get the most out of their resources. With your expertise you turn a combination of raw materials directly from nature and man-made components into powerful tools.
