Out of necessity, you've discovered passion. Ever since the implementation of agriculture into society, people have had the luxury of cooking. No more did all of a tribe's time have to be focused on collecting the food, now they can prepare it. You've taken a liking to this role, having learned recipes over time and even having developed a few of your own original flavor experiments. While certainly sustaining, food is not only that, but bonding as well. Eating together is a communal act, and preparing food for another is something akin to a gift, even if it is for pay. For pay, it usually is. You're lucky enough to be able to survive off of others's survival.

(Mostly) everyone has to eat. You prepare meals for others in exchange for coin. Luckily for you, you don't see the market going anywhere any time soon. Hopefully this isn't just a fad.
