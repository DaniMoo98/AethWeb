title: "Woodsman"
category: "Professional Classes"

There's nothing more satisfying than putting your hands to work and pulling together wood into something that can better the world around you. If there is, you haven't found it yet. After hours of practice, you've developed the skill to craft wood into useful tools and materials for things like weapons, shields and even buildings. While it's exhausting work, it's how you make a living. Whether it be to fashion a sturdy bow, made to last a decade, or to simple erect more cottages, you're where all the wood goes.

People expect a Woodsman to bring in lumber for refinement and put the wood collected in town to good use. They depend on you for shelter and other various items that they would not be able to get in any other material.
