# TRACKING & POINTS OF INTEREST

---

## Points of Interest

All POIs left outside of a clan's owned lands must contain the tracking information below. POIs placed inside your own clan with a negative effect on a clanmate (stealing, vandalism, heresy) must also include tracking info.

**For infiltration POIs, copy-paste the infiltration ticket number into any POI that results from said infiltration.**

By leaving a trackable POI, you consent to being contacted through Discord to resolve the tracking process. In the event of concerns, a tracking ticket should be opened.

### Tracking POI Format

- **Description of the Scene**
- **Discord Name**
- **Timestamp**
- **Date**
- **Tracking Modifier:** Half of Survival or Knowledge: Arcana (for magical POIs only), rounded up
- **Infiltration Ticket Number** (for infiltration POIs only)

When determining the value of your Survival or Knowledge: Arcana modifier, buffs and racial abilities may contribute so long as they were applied **PRIOR to or at the START** of the scene (e.g., Guidance, Fish Sandwiches).

When a POI is tracked (regardless of success or failure), the POI should be removed. If the POI content itself remains (a book in a library, a dug-up grave), the tracker should leave a secondary POI indicating it has been tracked. This is not required if the POI is older than 3 days.

### POI Difficulty Classes

If a POI includes a check without an outlined DC, it must fall within these bounds:

| DC | Difficulty |
|---|---|
| **1–10** | Easy. Common and mundane, never failed by a well-trained practitioner. |
| **11–15** | Moderate. A well-trained practitioner will seldom fail. |
| **15–20** | Hard. Failure is common even amongst the trained. |

## Types of POIs

### Flavor POIs

POIs left to impart information not directly related to combat or theft. Examples: details about an in-game object, scents, sounds, environmental effects, ritual effects, notes.

- Flavor POIs are subject to sensitive content rules — they cannot be used to gain automatic consent for NSFW content
- Flavor POIs for story building within your own base **do not** require tracking
- Vandalism POIs or interactions with another player's belongings **do** require tracking information

### Combat POIs

Required after any combat encounter, one per side (or per player heading to different locations):

- Brief description of the scene as it would appear after the fight
- Approximate number of combatants
- Whether magic was used (type only — Arcane, Divine, Primal — not every individual spell)

### Theft & Trespass POIs

Required when stealing, breaking into another clan's base, DM builds, or conducting internal IC betrayals. Tracking POIs are required for:

- Entry point into a build
- Exit point from a build
- Any breached, lockpicked, or Knocked doors
- Any broken chests/storage containers or mailboxes

Unlocked doors do not need a POI. Unlocked containers need a POI only if items are stolen.

**Mechanical Item Theft Cooldown:** If a player steals mechanical items, they are on a **3-day cooldown** from further theft from **any** clan. Stolen items must remain in inventory until tracking expires. During that period, the stolen items may be retrieved regardless of combat ratio. No stolen equipment may be equipped until the 3-day window concludes.

### Magical POIs

Left where magic effects or rituals are ongoing or have left their mark.

Required information:
- Effects on surroundings or upon others
- The Caster's Spell DC
- The School of Magic used
- The name of the Spell
- Ritual Ticket number (for ritual POIs)

Magic POIs can be dispelled using Dispel Magic, requiring Component Pouches equal to the ritual's cost and a successful 1d20+Spellcasting Modifier roll against the POI's Spell DC.

**Spell Detection DC:** 5 + 5 × the spell's mastery level

## POIs & Consent Zones

Players may establish a **Sensitive Content Zone** or **Consent Zone** for locations with implied mature content (dark sanctums, dungeons, etc.).

### Consent Zone Rules

- Must be in a secluded or private area — not visible from the exterior
- Activities must not be in a range higher than Say
- Zones can exist for standalone buildings, not entire clan builds
- In Forced Capture, consent is required to enter — players can't be forced to give consent
- You **cannot** store RPR items, silver, or DM/RP items in these zones

### Consent Zone POI Template

> **SENSITIVE CONTENT ZONE AHEAD**
> This area may contain adult / erotic roleplay.
> By entering, you agree to see adult themes. You cannot be forced to cross this barrier without your explicit consent.
> Themes included: (e.g., Torture, Violence, Murder)

**Covered by POI:** Nudity, violence/blood, murder of NPCs (sacrifices, thralls)

**NOT Covered by POI:** Non-consensual interactions, murder/torture of a PC, harm to non-clan-owned followers/NPCs

## TRACKING

### Tracking Overview

A character may track another from any POI left behind (combat, theft, escape) using either **Survival** or **Knowledge: Arcana** (for magical POI tracking).

Key rules:
- A character may only begin tracking **once every 20 hours** unless a perk allows more
- If leaving a group POI, only one member applies their tracking modifier
- Magical means of escape (Fly, Teleportation) require **Knowledge: Arcana** instead of Survival
- Dimension Door and Misty Step do **not** qualify for magical tracking
- All tracking must take place within **3 days** of POI placement

### How to Start Tracking

1. **Locate the POI** and contact the creator on Discord. You must wait **3 real-life hours** from POI placement before initiating tracking.
2. **Cannot track** while suffering from Medium wounds or greater.
3. **Roll** Survival (mundane) or Arcana (magical). The DC is determined by POI age + target's tracking modifier:

| POI Age | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Less than 1 day | 15 + modifier |
| 1 day | 17 + modifier |
| 2 days | 19 + modifier |
| 3+ days | Tracks dissipated — cannot track |

After 3 days, all tracking-related POIs must be removed when found.

4. **Screenshot** all rolls and emotes with timestamps (uncropped) and contact the player.

5. On success, the tracked player reveals the first **Region** their character moved to. Subsequent rolls continue until the final region is discovered (2 rolls minimum, 3 maximum).

6. **Final roll precision** based on how much you beat the DC:
   - **Beat DC by less than 5** → 3×3 grid
   - **Beat DC by 5+** → 2×2 grid
   - **Beat DC by 10+** → 1 tile grid

### Temporary Camps

A temporary camp can be registered with a build assistance ticket. A player who creates tracking to a camp must "return" to rest there each night for the 3-day tracking period. If not tracked, the camp can be torn down. If tracked, the player must make themselves available for RP or generate a secondary tracking POI to their regular base.

## LIMITS TO TRACKING

### Magical Tracking

Tracks left by magical means require **Knowledge: Arcana** proficiency. This includes magical flying (not physical flying).

### Demiplanes & Tracking

Players may **not** reside in a demiplane during tracking. They must rest in a temporary camp or clan build.

### Swimming

When a POI arises from someone fleeing by swimming, the tracking DC increases by **+1**.

### Wounds

Characters with **Medium or greater** injuries cannot track.

### Tracking in the Underdark

The Underdark is treacherous and difficult to track through:

- Tracking into/out of the Underdark always requires **3 rolls total**
- Tracking through the Underdark gives a **+1 DC** increase
- If both start and endpoint are in the Underdark, the tracker must roll **2 Regions** with the +1 DC increase
- Characters with the **Dungeon Delver** feat gain a **+1 bonus** to tracking in the Underdark
