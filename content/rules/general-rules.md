# Community Rules

> All rules are subject to revision. Changes will be announced in our Discord and updated here as soon as possible. Thank you for your understanding. — *Aethrya Staff*

---

## 1. Respectful Community

Aethrya is an inclusive roleplay community. We expect every member to treat others with respect, courtesy, and good faith — both in and out of character. We are here to build a world together, and that starts with how we treat each other. Aethrya maintains **zero tolerance for harassment** of any kind. Trolling, griefing, sexual harassment, bullying, and toxic behavior will be met with disciplinary action. While we generally do not police conduct outside our server, staff reserves the right to consider external context in extreme cases involving targeted harassment of Aethrya community members.

## 2. Zero Tolerance

Aethrya does not tolerate discrimination or harassment in any form — including sexism, racism, homophobia, transphobia, bigotry, or verbal abuse. Characters designed around fetishistic themes or offensive stereotypes are prohibited. Dishonesty toward staff within tickets is also a violation.

Retaliation against any player for filing a report or ticket is taken seriously. Directly or indirectly harassing, penalizing, or retaliating against a player due to a report outcome may result in a strike.

## 3. Keep It Drama-Free

Disagreements between players are inevitable, but escalating them is not. If you find yourself in a conflict with another player that you cannot resolve directly, we encourage you to step back and open a moderation ticket so staff can address the situation before it grows. Pitting players against one another or spreading out-of-character rumors about other players will not be tolerated.

## 4. OOC Channels Are SFW

All public out-of-character spaces — including global chat and the Aethrya Discord — are to remain safe for work. NSFW conversations must be kept to private channels or designated areas within Discord. This ensures all members feel comfortable participating in public discussion.

## 5. Sensitive Content

Aethrya permits the roleplay of dark and mature themes. We trust our players to handle these responsibly, but certain topics require extra care regarding all participants in a scene.

**Sensitive topics include but are not limited to:** graphic depictions of injury or torture, explicit sexual content, non-consensual sexual scenarios, or any combination thereof.

**Before engaging with sensitive content in RP, you must obtain explicit consent from all parties present in the scene.**

### Sensitive Content Requirements

When a scene progresses into sensitive territory, all participants must follow these guidelines:

- Consent must be obtained from every party present.
- Characters involved must either:
  - Move to a private, non-public location.
  - Choose a setting appropriate to the scene (e.g., a dungeon for interrogation, an infirmary for injury RP, a private residence for intimate scenes).
- Points of Interest (POIs) at your base that suggest adult content **do not replace the requirement for explicit consent.** POIs inform players of what RP to expect in a location, but direct consent must still be obtained for any graphic or sensitive emotes.
- **Public nudity is not permitted.** Characters must wear, at minimum, undergarments that fully cover the chest, bottom, and groin.
- Consent sheets via RP profiles in RolePlay Redux are available but optional. **These sheets do not override the requirement for in-scene consent.**

### Bystanders

If a player enters a scene involving sensitive content and does not wish to participate, they may leave at their earliest opportunity. The departing player should clearly communicate why they are leaving. As a courtesy, sensitive RP should use lower-range local channels (whisper or mumble).

### Scenes Involving Sexual Assault

Scenes containing any form of sexual assault may be roleplayed between consenting participants, but **the content must not leave that original scene.** This includes discussions of trauma, revenge, or any conversational mention. At no point may details from such a scene be shared beyond its original participants.

Sexual assault content — suggested or explicit — **may not be used as a plot device, backstory element, punishment, or vendetta.** Breaching the privacy of such scenes will result in immediate moderation, up to and including a permanent ban.

## 6. Prohibited Content

The following are **strictly prohibited** and will result in an immediate ban without appeal:

- **Pregnancy RP** — including using pregnancy as a consequence, abortion, miscarriage (forced, accidental, or consensual), or depiction of childbirth.
- **Underage characters** — all characters must be at least 20 years old. All elven characters must be at least 100 years old. Aethrya is an 18+ server; any character that appears or acts below adulthood will be addressed.
- **Bestiality or zoophilia** in any form.
- **Child sacrifice** in any form.

## 7. Metagaming

Metagaming — using out-of-character knowledge to influence in-character actions — is prohibited.

- **Soft meta** (consensual): Two players agree OOC to share information, such as arranging a meeting location. Both parties must consent.
- **Hard meta** (non-consensual): Using OOC information without the other party's knowledge or agreement. This is a rule violation and will be addressed by staff.

When responding to an LFRP with an advertised party size where combat may occur, you must match that number and not exceed it. You may only bring additional players with **explicit OOC consent** from the posting party, obtained before you approach the scene.

## 8. IC/OOC Separation

Keep in-character actions and emotions in character. Keep out-of-character matters out of character. Targeting players, or bleeding IC feelings into OOC interactions (or vice versa), is not permitted.

Remember: the characters are not the players. If you're having difficulty with another player, communicate directly and respectfully. If that fails, open a moderation ticket.

## 9. Whitelisting

All players must complete Aethrya's whitelisting process as outlined in Discord. Unwhitelisted players found outside the spawn area will face disciplinary action, up to an immediate ban. Sharing the whitelist passphrase with unwhitelisted players is also a violation.

The whitelist process verifies lore accuracy and confirms that each player has read and understood the rules. Additional requirements:

- Characters may not have direct divine intervention in their backstory (contacted, saved, or harmed by a deity directly). Agents or messengers of a deity are permitted.
- Backstories containing sensitive content must include a content warning at the beginning.
- **AI-generated backstories are not permitted.** If staff determines a backstory was AI-generated, the player may be denied or asked to rewrite it without generative AI.
- Characters may not be based on copyrighted material or themes. All writing and concepts must be original.
- After whitelisting, appearance changes (beyond hairstyle, makeup, and tattoos) require an appearance change ticket.

## 10. Roleplay Etiquette

Collaborative roleplay is about creating a story together — not about winning. We expect all players to practice good RP etiquette, especially in conflict and PvP encounters.

**Generative AI is banned from RP.** Using AI to generate part or all of your emotes may result in disciplinary action. Non-generative tools like Grammarly (for spelling and grammar) are permitted. Do not use AI to generate art of another player's character or a DMNPC without their express consent.

**Good etiquette includes:**
- Emoting into scenes upon approach
- Respecting the agency of other player characters
- Keeping OOC chatter to a minimum during scenes

**Good PvP etiquette includes:**
- Taking turns in a timely fashion
- Helping maintain turn order
- Supporting less experienced players with questions

## 11. Consent & Consequence

Consent and communication are the foundation of all RP on Aethrya. Consent information can be displayed through the Discord bot or via the bio tab in your Shift+R menu.

Before imposing consequences on another character, you are expected to check their consent sheet **and** communicate with the player OOC. For full details, see the [Consequence Rules](/rules/consequence-rules) page.

## 12. Social Skill Rolls

Social skills (persuasion, deception, intimidation, insight, etc.) represent how a character presents themselves — not mind control or lie detection.

- A high deception roll means the character maintained composure and revealed no tells. It does **not** mean the other character must believe the lie.
- Insight rolls detect subtle changes in demeanor. The target player should acknowledge the success or failure of the roll in their emote as best they can.

## 13. Always In Character

When you log in, your character is present in the world of Faerûn. Regardless of what's happening in your current storyline, you are always in character while on the server. This maintains immersion for everyone.

## 14. Streaming & Recording

To protect player privacy, prevent accidental metagaming, and avoid public exposure of mature RP content, **streaming of server roleplay is prohibited.** This includes all platforms — YouTube, Twitch, or any other live or recorded broadcast.

Do not record voice chats in Aethrya's official Discord servers without the consent of all parties being recorded.

## 15. Powergaming

All forms of powergaming are prohibited. Emotes directed at other players are **attempts**, not declarations. For example:

- ✅ *"Kira reaches for Darin's wrist."*
- ❌ *"Kira grabs Darin's wrist."*

We are here to create collaborative stories, not to dominate narratives. Examples of powergaming include:
- Emoting another player's actions or reactions
- Forcing a scene without consent
- Refusing to emote when another character addresses yours

## 16. RP Avoidance

RP avoidance — intentionally leaving a scene without responding or waiting for a reply — is not permitted. This includes walking away, teleporting out, or logging off mid-scene.

If you must leave, communicate clearly with the other players. **The only exception** is if the scene involves sensitive content you are uncomfortable with (see Bystanders rule above).

## 17. Exploitation

Intentional exploitation of any game system — whether Aethrya's custom systems or the base game engine — is strictly prohibited. We work hard to maintain balanced, fair gameplay, and exploiting bugs or glitches undermines that for everyone.

Examples include: glitching into inaccessible spaces, exploiting RPR bugs for advantage, admin shopping, impersonating another player's character, or using EE Packing to bypass build systems.

Discord plugins that bypass role permissions to view restricted channels are also considered exploitation.

## 18. Rule Lawyering

If you believe a rule has been broken or need clarification, open a moderation ticket. Do not "rule lawyer" other players directly — this frequently escalates into OOC conflict. Focus on the spirit of the rules, not technicalities.

## 19. Lore Accuracy

Aethrya strives for a world that is grounded in the Forgotten Realms setting. We ask that every player familiarize themselves with their race, class, and the setting lore. Meme roleplay and real-world references should be avoided.

Characters are not superheroes. Unrealistic feats, exceptionally rare concepts, or "special" character circumstances must be discussed with and approved by staff before play.

## 20. Catch-All

**20a.** Not every situation can be anticipated. If a violation falls within the spirit of an existing rule but isn't explicitly outlined, the moderation team may invoke this clause alongside the relevant rule.

**20b.** In cases where a player's actions don't technically break a rule but conflict with the server's principles, the admin team will convene and decide on a response. This may result in a new rule addition, which will be clearly communicated to the community.

## 21. Storage

All RPR items, resources, and coin must be stored either on your person or in a container that can be lockpicked or accessed by players outside your clan for infiltration purposes. Using storage that doesn't meet these criteria will result in disciplinary action.

## 22. Pillars of the Community

Staff are held to all rules at a higher standard. They are responsible not only for operating the server but for serving as role models within the community. If any staff member is witnessed intentionally breaking rules, players should open an admin ticket with evidence. No reprisal will result from good-faith reports.

# Discord Rules

## D1. Account Verification

Joining the Aethrya Discord will prompt account verification through Double Counter. Follow the bot's instructions via DM to receive access. If you encounter issues due to VPN or shared IP, open a ticket and staff will assist you.

## D2. No Alternative Accounts

Multiple Discord accounts in the server are not permitted. If staff discovers duplicate accounts, both will be banned.

## D3. No Server Promotion

Do not promote other Conan RP Discord servers in any channel. This is to prevent member fishing. Links to personal, professional, or non-Conan gaming Discords are allowed in appropriate channels only.

## D4. English Only

Aethrya's primary language is English. All posts in Discord channels should be in English for moderation purposes. Multilingual players are welcome, but public communication should remain in English.

## D5. No Spam

Rapid-fire posting, emoji spam, sticker spam, and GIF spam will be flagged. Deliberate spam will result in a warning or strike. Continued violations may lead to muting or further action.

## D6. No Controversial Topics

Politics, religion, body autonomy debates, extreme gore, and other inflammatory or disruptive topics are strictly prohibited in all Discord channels. Violations will result in immediate staff intervention.

## D7. Blocked Words

Certain words and phrases are automatically blocked. Attempting to circumvent filters with similar phrasing will result in a warning or strike.

## D8. Do Not Ping or DM Staff

Do not ping staff, modders, or strangers without conversational context (the reply function counts as context). Do not DM staff unless explicitly given permission within a ticket. Modders are treated as regular players — mod-related questions should go through their own Discords or Steam pages.

## D9. No NSFW Content

NSFW material is not allowed in the Aethrya Discord. Violations will result in content removal and consequences proportional to the offense.

## D10. No Unsolicited Messages

Do not send unsolicited DMs to community members. If you receive unwanted messages, open a ticket. Players found sending unwanted messages will face consequences.

## D11. Discord Terms of Service

All members must comply with [Discord's Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).

## D12. Art Theft

Aethrya takes art theft seriously. Posting stolen or traced artwork without proper credit to the original artist will result in disciplinary action.
