# BUILDING RULES

All players are required to be in a mechanical clan on Aethrya, even if playing solo. You must create your own solo clan or join a pre-existing clan.

Any player with a build/base found to be in violation of these rules will be immediately contacted in a ticket with defining instructions on what needs to change, as well as an established deadline by which the changes must be completed. This deadline will take into account the player's availability, and if the changes aren't made by the deadline, or discussed beforehand, the build will be subject to deletion or modification without further warning or reimbursement for lost items. If you're not sure if your base violates these rules or not, open a help ticket! The staff will be happy to review your build or concept and let you know.

If you are building for your clan or faction, you may input a tag above your head that says **'OOC Building'**. This will allow you to build without getting disturbed by potential roleplay. As you build, your character will be considered Out of Character and not present. Any characters **without** the 'OOC Building' status are still considered in character and may be approached according to our rules.

---

**Map Bounds**

There are to be no builds placed on the cliffs that border the map edges, or clipping through blocking volumes on the server. This can create load errors, leading to performance or other issues with the server. This is due to a limitation of Conan Exiles and the maximum allowed map size for the game itself.

**Performance Enhancer Mod**

Aethrya uses a performance enhancer mod to keep our database clean and manage high-population restarts. This mod periodically scrubs inventories of items that are not on a dedicated list of allowed storages. A full list of approved storages is accessible through the Storage Kit.

## i. PLAYER CLANS

Base build radius for player clans on the Surface is **80x80 tiles**, measured by the RPR move tool. In the Underdark, the base build radius is **60x60 tiles**. 1 tile is equal to 1 Conan foundation.

Player clans must be built at least **65 tiles** (using the RPR measuring tool) away, or **40 tiles** within the Underdark, from one another, edge-to-edge. To ease potential tensions, a distance of 100 or more tiles is advised.

Additionally, clan sizes will be limited to no more than **20 clan members**.

Main Factions, such as Leilon, Phandalin, and Sombersky, are not subject to the clan member limitations.

If you are the last or only person in your clan, you are required to delete your build before shelving/rerolling. If you are having any issues with this, please make a ticket ASAP before shelving/rerolling.

## ii. PLACES OF INTEREST

Various locations of the map contain Places of Interest. Places of Interest include Underdark entrances, Gathering Zones, and Warps. Player builds must be at least **50 tiles** (using the RPR measure tool) away from these places of interest. Any group/player found to be in violation of these rules will be given 48 hour notice to move their build or have it removed by Staff.

We do not allow players to build structures or place deco in the Upperdark, with the exception of POIs for the purposes of tracking.

## iii. TERRAIN

Players are allowed to build on interesting or unreachable terrain within limits, including an IC reason for being so. Player bases should appear architecturally sound, appropriate for the culture they represent, and functional for the region they're found in. No comically tall towers without adequate support, giant cubes, or honeycomb bases. Staying lore friendly and RP friendly is imperative to the server. If you are unsure, please open a ticket to discuss with staff.

## iv. TEMPORARY BUILDS

No building tiles or placeables may be placed outside a clan's main build area, with the exception of infiltrator notes, notes, and points of interest (POIs). Temporary camps may also be given an exception to this rule. To ask for a temporary build, please submit a building assistance ticket. Each request will be evaluated for the purpose of the temporary build to determine the amount of BP and decos allowed, as well as the length of time the temporary build may remain.

## v. LEAVE OF ABSENCE

Inactive clans/builds/characters will be deleted after a **four-week period** of inactivity. To prevent this from happening wrongfully, please open a ticket if you will be taking an extended leave of absence. The admins are happy to provide build kits should anyone return afterwards. This is to avoid clogging up the server when locations aren't being used.

If a player has a LOA with an unknown return time, the player may opt to inform staff and be allowed to shelf with their mastery marks to avoid character deletion and loss of progress.

## vi. HUB TERRITORY

Building in Hub territory is allowed and follows the same distance restrictions as building near player clans, listed above.

## vii. PATHWAYS

At Aethrya, immersion is a top priority. We require builds to steer clear of blocking pathways in the same way we require of Places of Interest such as Dungeons, Underdark caves, and Warps. This does not mean that all pathways are off limits. You may build on top of a pathway only if it is not the singular pathway to reach beyond the area. **You may only block a pathway if you occupy the endpoint of that specific pathway**, and only up to the 80 tile diameter limitation of your build.

## viii. SISTER CLANS

The creation of sister clans to circumvent the limited amount of players to a single clan is **NOT ALLOWED**. 'Sister clanning' is defined as a direct extension of another clan with the purpose of expanding membership of a single clan beyond the limits set in place for clan membership. Examples of this include having two clans under a single leadership structure, with communal resources.

## ix. RAVENCREST MAILBOXES AND BIRDS

**Mailboxes**

When not being used as a mailbox (such as operating as a deposit/donation box), mailboxes must have 'Can Receive Messages' unchecked. Clans may have **1 general mailbox** for use of the entire clan roster and **1 mailbox per individual clan member** that are capable of receiving mail. All other mailboxes must have 'Can Receive Messages' unchecked.

Mailboxes found in person in a player's base that have a name on them can be used as IC knowledge of that player's name, clan, or location. Mailboxes found on the mailing list do **not** count as IC information.

**Birds**

Clans are permitted **two (2)** Ravencrest Courier birds to be used as placeables for the purpose of the alert function. Birds that are nested in mailboxes, used as shoulder pets, or functioning as non-alert placeables are not included in this count.

## x. DOORS AND CHESTS

All doors and chests must be able to be lockpicked. Some mods do not communicate with our lock system —  please verify compatibility before placing them.

## xi. EE PACKING

EE Packing is strictly forbidden, as it bypasses our BP and placeables limits. If you are caught using this, it falls under our Exploitation rule and will incur consequences.

## xii. EXCESSIVE PLACEABLE USE

Players should be mindful of the performance impact of their build. This means they should not stack placeables in excessive densities, or use excessive amounts of torches or lighting, as doing so creates performance issues for other players.

Additionally, players should be conscientious of their use of ToT Mannequins with accessories, as they load client side and may cause rooms to appear empty on lower-end machines.

If you are unsure if something is excessive or wish for advice on reducing lag, please feel free to open a building assistance ticket.

## xiii. UNDERWATER BUILDING

A base on land or a partially submerged base that utilizes waterways giving the Swimming status **MUST** have visible and accessible ways to exit the water.

Waterblock foundations or water planes do not trigger Aquatic Combat or Aquatic Base infiltration rules. These rules are only instituted by waterways that trigger the Swimming status.

## xiv. CLAN STORAGE IN UNDERWATER BUILDS

Any base that has a majority (more than half) of its livable structure beneath the water is considered partially submerged. A structure must be considered partially submerged or fully submerged in order to keep clan valuables storage under water.

Players cannot keep a vault/storage room as their only underwater space solely to make infiltration harder — this is an exploitation of the rule.

## xv. SECRET DOORS

Secret/hidden doors — including those from the Bazaar and/or DLC — require a POI if the door is locked. The POI must have a minimum radius of 2 outlining a **DC12 (Investigation)** for others to discover a locked door. Whether locked or not, secret doors **MUST** be physically accessible.

# BUILDING LIMITS

**Note:** Vanilla Conan Exiles crafting systems are not necessary for this server's gameplay. Kits will be given to players for building, as the server has `/cheat nocost` implemented.

### BUILDING EXTENSION

Purchase a building extension for **20 gold / 2000 silver**. This extends both the tile and placeable allowance by 500 each. Enter a help ticket to purchase this extension. One extension allowed per clan.

### DONATE

Support the server by becoming a donor and redeem your rewards by entering a ticket. Tile and placeable extension amounts vary by donor package. See **Donation Tiers & Milestones** for more info.
